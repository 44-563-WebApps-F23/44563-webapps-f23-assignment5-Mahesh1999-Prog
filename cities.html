This makes the city names less flexible in adjusting to smaller screen sizes, causing them to break into a new row earlier than the letters.
  • Hence, It's important to note that the behavior observed is due to the specific Bootstrap classes applied to the columns. The choice of "col-sm-2" for the letters and "col-md" for the city names determines when they wrap to a new row based on screen size breakpoints.
